"Finding God" by Father Daniel Bedel

Father Daniel Bedel, a young vibrant priest with relation in the parish, will be giving our Lenten Mission. Sunday night's talk will take place at St. Maurice, Napoleon and is entitled "Finding God Within Us". Fr. Dan will explore balance in our lives, being centered in prayer, and discovering the person God created us to be. Monday night's talk will take place here at Immaculate Conception, Millhousen and is entitled "Finding God Outside of Us". In this talk he will discuss mindfulness, appreciating the simple things, a renewed sense of gratitude, and rituals in out lives.

  • March 5th & 6th
  • 7 PM
  • Sunday: St. Maurice, Napoleon
  • Monday: Immaculate Conception, Millhousen
