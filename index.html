    Quarantine Easter Tips
    Pull out the good china and put on your best Easter attire. Easter is a time to celebrate! No, things won't be quite the same this year. It's going to feel weird not going to church on Easter Sunday. But the reason for the season hasn't changed!

    Here are some ways to make the most out of your Easter this year:
    1. Tune in to Mass via live stream at https://bit.ly/2V3OZQV.
    2. Go ahead and wear your Easter best!
    3. Listen to your favorite music on an Easter playlist.
    4. Schedule a virtual gathering with your family and friends to wish them a Happy Easter.
    5. Make a donation that is meaningful to you, such as your parish or your favorite Catholic charity or organization. 
    6. Send out Easter cards to loved ones and/or those who are home bound.
    7. Dye Easter eggs and then hide them around your yard for the kids to find.
    8. Easter is a celebration after all, so set the tone for the day by decorating your table and pulling out the good china.
    9. Use this at-home Easter as an excuse to start a new tradition with your family.
    We would love to see how your family celebrates this year, and of course see you in your Easter best! Click on the link below to share your Easter photos and/or video with us. Or post them on your Facebook page and use the hashtag #archindycares. We will make a fun video collage and share it so everyone can enjoy.
